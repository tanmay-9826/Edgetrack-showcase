The Challenges We Addressed

Current public transport systems leave commuters in the dark and authorities without data.

No Real-Time Visibility

Commuters wait unpredictably without accurate tracking or estimated arrival times.

Overcrowding & Delays

Inefficient route utilization leads to packed buses and missed schedules.

Poor Connectivity

Target areas often suffer from intermittent or no internet access.

High Cost & Exclusion

Solutions often target big cities, excluding rural feature phone users.

The Technical Solution

A low-cost, scalable architecture designed for small city constraints.

📡

High-Precision Hardware

Neo-M8N GPS + ESP32 modules provide satellite-level tracking for under ₹1,000.

📶

Low-Bandwidth Protocols

MQTT + Protobuf for data efficiency, with SMS/IVR offline fallbacks.

🤖

Edge AI Processing

TinyML on buses predicts ETA and stops offline without active internet.

SmartFleet Management Dashboard

The central control center for real-time fleet operations.

Project Impact

Social Benefits

  • Safe transport for women & elderly
  • Reduced waiting anxiety
  • Emergency SOS alerts

Economical

  • Saves ~15-30 mins daily
  • Reduces private vehicle fuel costs
  • Opens ad/partnership scope

Educational

  • Punctual arrival for students
  • Reduced transport delays
  • Boosts local digital literacy
