The Challenges We Addressed

Current public transport systems leave commuters in the dark and authorities without data. We set out to fix the core issues.

No Real-Time Visibility

Commuters wait unpredictably at bus stops without accurate tracking or estimated arrival times.

Overcrowding & Delays

Inefficient route utilization leads to packed buses, frustrated passengers, and missed schedules.

Poor Connectivity

Many target areas suffer from intermittent or no internet access, breaking traditional tracking apps.

High Cost & Exclusion

Current solutions target big cities and smartphone users, entirely excluding the feature phone population.

The Technical Solution

A low-cost, highly scalable architecture designed specifically for the constraints of small cities.

📡

High-Precision Hardware

Utilizing the low-cost Neo-M8N GPS module with ESP32 microcontrollers for satellite-level location tracking under ₹1,000.

📶

Low-Bandwidth Protocols

Leveraging MQTT + Protobuf for highly efficient data transmission, backed by an SMS & IVR offline fallback system.

🤖

Edge AI Processing

Running TinyML directly on the buses to predict the next stop and ETA, even when internet connectivity drops entirely.

Project Impact

Social Benefits

  • Safer transport for women & elderly
  • Instant alerts for breakdowns/emergencies
  • Reduced waiting anxiety

Economical

  • Saves 15–30 minutes daily per commuter
  • Increases transport revenue via adoption
  • Reduces private vehicle fuel costs

Educational

  • Helps students reach classes on time
  • Reduces transport-related absenteeism
  • Boosts digital literacy in small cities
